Detailed Itinerary:

Day 1:

Arrive at your pickup location our representative will welcome you. Drive to Kanatal Camp. Arrivals at campsite, Welcome drink (Non Alchoholic) on arrival Get freshen up and get your self-Acclimatized. Post Acclimatization, get ready for Adventurous Activities Rock climbing, Rappelling, Valley crossing, Burma Bridge etc. Return back to the camp till lunch. Post lunch free to play Ground Sports Activities or Indoor Games like Volley Ball, Football, Badminton, Cricket, Chess, Tambola and Carom etc. Evening at leisure & after sunset bonfire/campfire and bar-be-que snacks after bonfire Enjoy the dinner. Overnight stay in Luxury Tents on twin sharing basis.

Day 2:

Morning Wake Up Call at 6:00 am. Morning Tea & snacks, trekking to acclimatize yourself. After Breakfast, Checkout from the Camp & drive to your destinations. On the way you may visit any of the nearby local attractions. This brings an end to the trip, head back with lots of happy memories and moments that will be cherished for a lifetime.

Safety

As far as the safety and security is concerned the camp is absolutely safe having sufficient staff and our experienced Team members working 24 hours with security guards in and around the camp. Area is free from any danger species of animals. All adventure activities are conducted with utmost care under guidance of professional instructors. All our instructors are having long experience in adventure activities.

About the Place:

Location: Kanatal is a small village in the state of  Uttarakhand, India. Kanatal is 78 km from Dehradun  (capital of the State Uttarakhand), 38 km from  Mussoorie and 12 km from  Chamba. It is on the Chamba-Mussoorie road and nearly 300 km from Delhi.
